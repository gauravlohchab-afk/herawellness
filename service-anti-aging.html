Anti-Aging Treatment

Home / Services / Anti-Aging Treatment

Ageing is inevitable — but how gracefully you age is now a choice. Our anti-ageing treatments use the most advanced non-invasive technologies to lift, firm, and redefine facial contours, minimise fine lines, and restore the density and vitality of your skin.

About Anti-Aging Treatment

Our anti-ageing protocols combine cosmetic acupuncture, radio frequency skin tightening, peptide-rich infusions, and targeted collagen induction therapies. Each treatment is calibrated to your skin's age, condition, and goals — delivering natural-looking results without surgery or downtime.

Key Benefits

  • Visible reduction of fine lines and wrinkles
  • Facial lifting and contouring
  • Improved skin firmness and elasticity
  • Deeper hydration and plumpness
  • Restored skin density and volume
  • Natural, non-surgical results

What to Expect: Procedure Overview

  1. Ageing assessment and goal-setting consultation
  2. Targeted cleansing and exfoliation
  3. Radio frequency or microcurrent lifting
  4. Cosmetic acupuncture (where indicated)
  5. Peptide and stem cell infusion
  6. LED red light for collagen stimulation

Expected Results

Clients typically notice a visible lift and improvement in skin firmness after 3–4 treatments. The full programme of 8 sessions produces results comparable to early surgical intervention, with no downtime.
