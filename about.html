Our Story

Inspired by Hera — goddess of renewal

Hera Wellness Studio was founded with a single vision: to create a sanctuary where beauty, health, and genuine self-care converge. Inspired by Hera — the goddess of strength, renewal, and feminine vitality — we opened our doors to redefine what a wellness studio can be.

What started as a boutique wellness haven has grown into a studio celebrated for personalised care, evidence-based treatments, and an unwavering commitment to helping every client look and feel their very best. Be one of our Heranians.

  • Founded in Brisbane, 2016
  • Over 2,000 clients served
  • Certified dermatology & wellness specialists
  • Award-winning client satisfaction
